Type of cloud services


There are three main types of cloud computing which are built on top of one another and called computing “stack”. They provide various levels of flexibility and control for the management of the cloud computing system.


Infrastructure as a Service

IaaS provides infrastructure as service. Clients can access to lowest layers of the computer system. A well-designed infrastructure will be set for our clients through the internet. Users just need to handle the application, data, runtime, middleware and operating system, while we take care the rest.


Platform as a Service

PssA provide platform or runtime environment for application development. It eliminates the need of users to manage or maintain the underneath architecture. They could put forth their greatest effort to manage their applications and data.


Software as a Service

SaaS will provide a product or software to the users. The on-demand software allows users to interact on cloud-based apps. For example, Gmail is one of the famous SaaS mail provider. SaaS has been widely utilized in Marketing, Customer Relationship Management, eCommerce, etc. It provides solutions and convenient services for different business models.
